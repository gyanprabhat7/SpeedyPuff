About

    The Speedy Puff is an arcade-style endless car racing game in which the player controls the car,
    having the goal of not crashing with other vehicles. The player is tasked with navigating the car
    through 5 lane highway. Cars are randomly spawned on the map
    which then randomly changes their lane.
    Players get Points as they advance
    Crashing with other cars will send you flying and end the gameplay.
